### How to process these in Audacity

1. Hit record and read all 15 sentences one after the other, leaving a 2-second pause between each.
2. Once done, highlight each sentence individually and export them as `cmd_002.wav`, `cmd_003.wav`, etc., so they are cleanly separated.
3. Don't worry about background noise too much; having a little bit of natural room noise actually helps the model generalize better to real-world conditions.
